LCD Video Wall Display

A video wall consists of multiple display screens usually in a (2×2) (3×3) & (4×3) format. These screens are usually placed on top of each other or side by side. It allows using the overall screen size of all available devices to create a large screen. Video wall screens are available in different sizes 46” & 55”. The LCDs that are used for video walls are preferred to be thin bezel so that the space between the LCDs is less visible and as a result gives a better view when all the screens are tile together.
